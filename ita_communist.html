3. Lore & Geopolitical Goals

Background: The Italy of 2025 is gripped by economic crisis, social tensions, and democratic instability. The Communist parties are organizing in factories and public squares. The radical Left views revolution as the only way to change the system and build a model alternative to NATO and the EU.

Short-term Goals: Occupy the factories, consolidate the working-class base, and transform political power.

Long-term Goals: Build The International, export the revolution, create the European Soviet Republic or the Revolutionary Republic.

Principal Rivals: France, Germany, Russia, USA/NATO, and the moderate Italian forces.
