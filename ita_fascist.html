3. Lore & Geopolitical Goals

Background: Italy in 2025 is gripped by an economic crisis, massive immigration, internal tensions, and European instability. The neo-fascist forces of CasaPound push for a "Second March on Rome," while the House of Savoy dreams of a monarchical restoration. Rome looks to the East and South with growing apprehension.

Short-term goals: stabilize the nation, control internal dissent, and define the future between NATO alignment and national self-reliance.

Long-term goals: rebuild the Italian Social Republic, claim Istria and Dalmatia, withdraw from NATO, and build a sphere of influence in the Mediterranean.

Main rivals: France, Germany, Austria, Croatia/Slovenia, Russia, USA/NATO, Vatican.
